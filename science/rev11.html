Questions

1. How are species related to biodiversity?
Answer: In a biodiversity, there are multiple different species.

2. How do the characteristics of living things contribute to an organism's survival?
Answer: An organism needs these characteristics, because if they didn't, they couldn't live in the first place.

3. Describe the relationship between cells and organisms.
Answer: All organisms are made up of cells.

4. How does biodiversity depend on an organism's ability to reproduce?
Answer: If an organism can reproduce, the offspring might gain a mutation that will help it survive better through natural selection. Or, it will allow for the continuation of the species' survival so it does not become extinct.
