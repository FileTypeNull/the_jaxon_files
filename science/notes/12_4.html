Notes

Microbes Have Changed the Physical and Chemical Composition of Earth=
  • Scientists have found evidence that photosynthetic life evolved more than 3.5 billion years ago, since that is the age of the oldest known fossils
  • These fossils are of a group of marine cyanobacteria, which are bacteria that can carry out photosynthesis
  • Like all early life forms, each cyanobacterium was a single prokaryotic cell
  • Some cyanobacteria live in colonies and form stromatolites
Eukaryotic Cells May Have Evolved Through Endosymbiosis=
  • Endosymbiosis is a relationship in which one organism lives within the body of another, and both benefit from the relationship
