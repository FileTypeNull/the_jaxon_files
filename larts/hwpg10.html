Shakespearean Sonnet

There once was a fair knight holding a sword.
He slayed a dragon with his own bare hands.
His rank changed into that which is a lord,
And everyone followed all his demands

A wizard came into the town to say:
"This town shall now be mine to sell and buy,
If you, indeed, set forth into the day,
I shall make actions in which you will die."

The people were not happy, which was bad
Because they started rioting to tell:
"You can't do that to us, it's very sad;
We can't believe you made us have to yell!"

However, the wizard was very mad.
The people died which was very bad.
