1984 - Analysis #1

Quotes

  1. "It was one of those pictures which are so contrived that the eyes follow you when you move. BIG BROTHER IS WATCHING YOU, the caption beneath it ran" (Orwell 1-2)
  2. "It was the Police Patrol, snooping into people windows ... Only the Thought Police mattered" (Orwell 2)
  3. "But it was no use, he could not remember: nothing remained of his childhood except a series of bright-lit tableaux, occurring against no background and mostly unintelligible" (Orwell 3)
  4. "WAR IS PEACE, FREEDOM IS SLAVERY, IGNORANCE IS STRENGTH" (Orwell 4)
  5. "... but if detected it was certain that it would be punished by death, or at least by twenty-five years in a forced labor camp" (Orwell 6)

Analysis

  1. This quote represents the totalitarianism characteristic of a dystopian society
  2. This symbolizes the oppression characteristic of a dystopian society
  3. In this statement, Orwell expresses how Winston cannot remember his past due to brainwashing
  4. This slogan represents that the government discourages freedom and demands totalitarian control
  5. This represents government oppression, which is a characteristic of a dystopian society
