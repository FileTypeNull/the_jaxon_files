Story

Daniel, a high school junior, woke up and saw the sun near its highest point in the sky. When he saw the time, he jumped out of bed and honed his vision on the clock. It was 11:28 in the morning! He cursed and ran into his bathroom, getting dressed and washed up for school. Daniel started wishing he hadn't taken that opiate last night to help him fall asleep. He drowsily ran down the stairs, took a muffin from the kitchen counter, got in his car, and drove to school. Daniel's antiquated car puffed and moaned all the way to school, eventually letting out a small hiss when he turned off the ignition. He gorged his muffin down and ran into the building, rushing toward his 4th period class. On the way, he saw a vending machine and decided he could use a soft drink. However, this act was wasting time! This caused lackadaisical Daniel to miss his 4th period class, and having to deal with making up his Math Test. At this point, he was feeling quite demented after that mishap.
